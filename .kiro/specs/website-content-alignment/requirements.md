# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This feature aligns the aiseo.hamburg website content and structure with the updated landing page content document. The document defines 10 sections with specific German copy optimized for SEO, GEO, AEO, and AI-citability. The hero section visual structure is kept as-is, but all content needs alignment with the new copy.

## Glossary

- **Website**: The aiseo.hamburg React single-page application
- **Section**: A distinct visual and content block on the website
- **CTA**: Call-to-action button or link
- **AI_SEO**: Optimization for AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Hero Section Content Update

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to see the correct hero messaging, so that I immediately understand the value proposition.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Website SHALL display the H1 "Dein Unternehmen wird von ChatGPT empfohlen. Oder eben nicht."
2. THE Website SHALL display the subline "AI SEO für kleine und mittlere Unternehmen in Hamburg..."
3. THE Website SHALL display the intro text about customers asking ChatGPT instead of Google
4. THE Website SHALL include primary CTA "AI-Sichtbarkeit kostenlos prüfen"
5. THE Website SHALL include secondary CTA "So funktioniert's ↓"

### Requirement 2: Problem Section

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to understand why classic SEO isn't enough, so that I see the need for AI SEO.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Website SHALL include a section titled "Warum klassische SEO allein nicht mehr reicht"
2. THE Website SHALL explain "Google zeigt 10 Links. ChatGPT gibt eine Antwort."
3. THE Website SHALL list three AI evaluation criteria: klar erklärt, eindeutig eingeordnet, konsistente strukturierte Informationen
4. THE Website SHALL state "AI SEO schließt diese Lücke."

### Requirement 3: Mechanism Section

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to understand how ChatGPT decides which companies to mention.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Website SHALL include a section titled "Wie ChatGPT entscheidet, wen es empfiehlt"
2. THE Website SHALL display a table with three questions: Wer?, Für wen?, Wo und wann?
3. THE Website SHALL state "Entscheidend ist Klarheit, nicht Bekanntheit."

### Requirement 4: Benefits, Proof, and Differentiation Sections

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to understand the concrete benefits and what makes this service different.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Website SHALL include a benefits section titled "Was dir AI-Sichtbarkeit konkret bringt" with three benefits: Vorqualifiziert, Intentionsstark, Weniger Streuverlust
2. THE Website SHALL include a proof section titled "Funktioniert AI SEO auch für kleine Unternehmen?" confirming small businesses often do better
3. THE Website SHALL include a differentiation section with comparison table: Klassische Agentur vs AI SEO Hamburg

### Requirement 5: Offer, Process, Objections, Closing, and FAQ Sections

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to understand the service offering and next steps, so that I can take action.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Website SHALL include an offer section titled "AI-Sichtbarkeits-Check für Hamburger Unternehmen" with four check points: Ist-Zustand, Ursachenanalyse, Potenzial-Einschätzung, Handlungsempfehlung
2. THE Website SHALL include a process section with three steps: Kurzes Gespräch, Analyse aus KI-Perspektive, Klare Empfehlung
3. THE Website SHALL include an objections section titled "Für wen AI SEO nicht geeignet ist" with three exclusion criteria
4. THE Website SHALL include a closing section with CTA "AI-Sichtbarkeit kostenlos prüfen"
5. THE Website SHALL include an FAQ section with schema-optimized questions and answers
